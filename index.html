Why Andrex?

Trusted and loved since 1942

We’ve been making toilet tissue since 1942 and have been market leaders since 1961. Even the Queen is a fan. Andrex was awarded the Royal Warrant in 1978 to indicate we’re a regular supplier of toilet tissue to the Royal households.

We believe everyone deserves to feel clean

We have partnered with leading global aid organisations to bring sanitation around the world changing thousands of lives through projects with Unicef and Water Aid. Most recently, we have supported helping those who need our products with InKind Direct.

Always innovating to make things better

We know how important the environment is to you and your family. So we’re doing our bit to be more sustainable – whether that’s introducing paper packaging, using 66% less water when making Andrex or simply continuing to innovate and update our tissue fibre sourcing.
